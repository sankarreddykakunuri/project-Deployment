History of Hermès Handbags

Founded in 1837, Hermès began as a harness workshop in Paris and has since evolved into a symbol of luxury. Their handbags, especially the iconic Birkin and Kelly models, are revered for their craftsmanship, exclusivity, and timeless appeal. Vintage Hermès handbags are highly collectible and often appreciate in value over time.

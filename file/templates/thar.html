History of Mahindra Thar

The Mahindra Thar is the modern-day successor to the iconic Willys Jeep, which played a crucial role during World War II. First launched in India in 2010, the Thar quickly became a favorite among off-road enthusiasts. The 2021 model introduces modern features like enhanced safety, improved comfort, and superior performance while preserving the rugged nature that has defined the vehicle for generations.
